Down Payment
Assistance Programs

01

NCHFA Home Loan

The NC Housing Finance Agency offers below-market interest rates and down payment assistance to eligible first-time and move-up buyers across North Carolina.

Learn More
02

Chenoa Down Payment

A nationwide program providing down payment assistance in the form of a forgivable or repayable second mortgage — helping buyers cover the 3.5% FHA requirement.

Learn More
03

Community Partners Loan Pool

Deferred, forgivable loans for income-qualifying buyers purchasing in designated neighborhoods. A powerful tool for buyers who need help with closing costs.

Learn More
04

USDA Loans

Zero-down-payment mortgages for eligible rural and suburban buyers. Many areas around Charlotte qualify — ask Frank if your target neighborhood is eligible.

Learn More
05

Credit Repair Guidance

Not quite ready to buy? Frank connects clients with trusted resources to repair and rebuild credit so you can qualify for the best rates possible.
